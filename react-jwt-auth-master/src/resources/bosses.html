BOSSES TEAMS CONTACT

DVALIN

FLY-BY

Dvalin disappears from view, then swoops through the center of the platform, dealing a massive amount of damage


WHAT TO DO

Move to the edge of platform, or time your dodge when his claws hit the bridge

DRAGON BREATH

Dvalin lands on the player's side of the platform and unleashes a powerful breath between his feets


WHAT TO DO

After Dvalin lands, dash towards the other side of the platform. Deal as much damage as possible to his talons to whittle away his shield.

BITE

Dvalin bites towards the area the player are standing on. It is a quick attack that deals moderate damage.


WHAT TO DO

stand at the center front of the platform, the rear of the platform, or stay on the wind current. It is possible to deal damage to both the talons and his head after he attacks, but there is a small window

PULSE BOMB

Dvalin shoots two pairs of Pulse Bomb towards you, exploding upon impact.


WHAT TO DO

The attack is aimed but not homing, so it can be dodged by continuously moving in one direction.

ENERGY RAIN

Dvalin unleashes a rain of Pulse Bombs to attack a group of platforms you are standing on. The affected platforms deal continuous damage to the character on the field. The longer the character stands on the platform, the higher the damage received.


WHAT TO DO

Move to the nearest unaffected platform

OMEGA BOMB

Dvalin fly to the other end of the arena and begin charging. He then unleashes a gigantic pulse bomb towards the platform. It deals massive damage to the entire platform.


WHAT TO DO

Stay above the platform using the wind current to avoid damage.

ENERGY LIGHTNING

Periodically, there will be circular indicators on the ground showing where the energy pulse will strike (actual radius is not accurately defined). These bolts deal low-moderate damage, but failing to dodge them will whittle away your HP.


WHAT TO DO

DPS check. Dodge and hurry and finish off Dvalin.
